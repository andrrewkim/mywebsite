My Works

ProdCon 2022 PM x
Currant App Design

This year, I was able to attend ProdCon where I was able to
design an app for Social Currant, a youth-powered media
agency building a platform to connect creators with brands
for advertising partnerships. We designed this hi-fidelity
prototype as well as a pitch slide deck to address the pain
points and make the process for influencer marketing
more efficient and fast.

Food Magazine
Design Project

A food magazine I designed for one of my GBDA
final projects – I told my journey and story of my
relationship with food growing up in three stages:
childhood, adolescence, and adulthood. While keeping
the theme of a food design/cookbook style, I also
touched on my ethnic and cultural identity with food.
